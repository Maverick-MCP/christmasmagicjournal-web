Privacy Policy

Christmas Magic Journal — Last updated: March 19, 2026

Overview

Christmas Magic Journal is designed with your privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or have access to any of your personal data.

Data Storage

All of your journal entries, settings, and preferences are stored exclusively on your device and in your private iCloud account. We never have access to your iCloud data — it is encrypted and stored in your private database by Apple.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No usage data collection
  • No crash reporting that includes personal information
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data

iCloud Sync

Christmas Magic Journal uses Apple's iCloud to sync your journal entries privately across your iPhone and iPad. This sync happens entirely through Apple's infrastructure using your personal iCloud account. We have no access to this data.

On-Device AI Processing

If you enable the AI Companion feature, all processing happens entirely on your device using Apple's on-device intelligence. Your journal entries never leave your device for AI processing.

Journaling Suggestions

Christmas Magic Journal integrates with Apple's Journaling Suggestions framework (iOS 17.2+). Journaling suggestions are generated by iOS on your device. We do not access or store the underlying data — only what you choose to include in your journal entry.

Photos

When you add photos to your journal entries, they are embedded directly in your entry data and stored on your device and in your private iCloud account. Photos are never uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties.

Children's Privacy

Christmas Magic Journal does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app contains no objectionable content and is rated 4+.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the changes on this page and update the date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please visit our support page or open an issue on our GitHub repository.
